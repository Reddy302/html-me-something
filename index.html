Caticorns are Meowgical!

If you have never encountered one of these illustrious creatures, read on to learn more!

Anatomy of a Caticorn

Caticorns are mythical creatures that come in different shapes, sizes, and colors. Here are some of the most common characteristics of these meowgical creatures.

Head

The head of a Caticorn is very similar to the head of a cat. Below are some of the differences you might find.

  • Unicorn Horn
  • Mane
    • Some Caticorns have brightly colored manes. Some lack this feature.

Body

The body of a Caticorn is almost exactly the same as the body of a standard cat. Some Caticorns have a brightly colored mohawk-like stripe extending from mane to tail.

Tail

Some Caticorns have brightly colored tails that resemble a standard cat tail. Others have tails that resemble that of a unicorn which are also brightly colored.

Where can I find a Caticorn?

  • In your dreams. These elusive creatures are very good at hiding. A person may go their entire lives without ever seeing a Caticorn. If the right ritual is performed before laying down to rest, one might be lucky enough to see a real Caticorn.
  • Over the rainbow. Most people will never have the chance to make it over the rainbow. If you are lucky enough to find yourself on the other side of a rainbow, (you will know that the side is "other" when you see a leprechaun's pot of gold) you will still find the task of finding a Caticorn daunting; but don't fret, they are there!
  • Within the spectrum of light. When a beam of light is split into its spectrum, magic happens. Caticorns can be found anywhere that magic exists. The light spectrum is a Caticorn's true playground.
